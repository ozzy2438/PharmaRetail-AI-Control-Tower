About the role

The AI Engineer is responsible for delivering high quality engineering outcomes against agreed objectives and working interdependently on shared team goals.
This role is a hands-on technical delivery role focused on building, deploying and operating enterprise grade AI capabilities in production. The role is primarily accountable for hands-on engineering delivery, platform contribution and reliable BAU outcomes.
Key Responsibilities

• Build and operate enterprise LLM/GenAI solutions integrated with Snowflake, dbt Cloud and approved cloud services.
• Design and implement RAG capabilities over governed enterprise data and documentation.
• Deliver secure conversational AI and agentic AI solutions with appropriate guardrails and governance controls.
• Implement LLMOps/MLOps and CI/CD pipelines for AI applications, including monitoring, evaluation and deployment automation.
• Monitor and optimise AI solution quality, reliability, latency and cost.
• Partner with data engineering and business stakeholders to deliver AI products aligned to enterprise data models and business outcomes.
• Contribute to reusable AI engineering standards, templates and best practices across the organisation.

## Aranan Teknik Yetkinlikler

İlan üç ana katmanda yetkinlik istiyor:

**1. AI/LLM Mühendisliği**
- RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) mimarisi kurma deneyimi
- Konuşmaya dayalı AI (conversational AI) ve agentic AI çözümleri
- LLMOps/MLOps: model izleme, değerlendirme (evaluation), deployment otomasyonu
- AI çözüm kalitesi, gecikme (latency) ve maliyet optimizasyonu

**2. Veri Mühendisliği**
- Snowflake: güvenlik, performans optimizasyonu, maliyet yönetimi (sadece sorgu yazmak değil, platform yönetimi seviyesinde)
- dbt Cloud: veri modelleme, ortam yönetimi, Git workflow'ları
- Güçlü SQL ve veri modelleme temelleri (dimensional/semantic modeling)

**3. Yazılım Mühendisliği Disiplini**
- Temiz kod pratikleri, test yazma, paketleme (packaging) ile Python
- CI/CD pipeline'ları (data ve AI ürünleri için)
- Otomatik test türleri: unit, integration, data quality, regression
- Güvenlik/governance: RBAC/RLS, masking, logging

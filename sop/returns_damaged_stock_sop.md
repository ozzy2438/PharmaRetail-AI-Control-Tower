## [RD-01] Classification

Classify stock as customer return, delivery damage, in-store damage, expired stock, recall stock, or cold-chain quarantine. Recall and cold-chain stock must follow their dedicated SOPs and cannot be processed as ordinary damaged stock.

## [RD-02] Segregation

Remove non-saleable stock from the sales floor, label the reason and date, and place it in the approved secure holding area. Prevent the item from being replenished, transferred, or sold while disposition is pending.

## [RD-03] Recording requirements

Record store, product, quantity, reason code, source transaction or delivery, condition, operator, and timestamp. Photographs may be retained only where permitted and must not contain customer or prescription information.

## [RD-04] Disposition and credit

Use only approved return-to-supplier, destruction, donation, or write-off routes. Supplier credit claims must reference the delivery and damage evidence. Staff must not promise credit outside authorised commercial terms.

## [RD-05] Reconciliation

The physical quantity moved to holding must equal the inventory adjustment and final disposition quantity. Investigate mismatches before incident closure and escalate suspected loss or repeated delivery damage.

## [IA-01] Permitted reasons

Inventory adjustments require an approved reason: verified count variance, damage, expiry, recall, cold-chain disposition, authorised transfer correction, or receiving correction. An adjustment must never be used to hide a replenishment, delivery, or system error.

## [IA-02] Verification

Perform a physical count and check open receipts, transfers, returns, quarantined stock, recent sales, and previous adjustments. A second authorised person must verify high-value, controlled, repeated, or threshold-exceeding adjustments.

## [IA-03] Posting rule

Record signed adjustment quantity, reason, evidence reference, operator, approver when required, and event timestamp. The inventory equation must remain opening stock plus received quantity minus sold quantity plus or minus adjustments equals closing stock.

## [IA-04] Escalation

Escalate repeated discrepancies, unexplained negative variance, suspected loss, post-recall sales, or adjustments that would create negative inventory. Negative closing inventory is prohibited.

## [IA-05] Audit and review

Daily exception review must identify large, repeated, or unapproved adjustments. Retain count evidence and linked incident identifiers. Corrections must be additive audit events rather than deletion of the original record.

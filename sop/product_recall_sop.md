## [PR-01] Recall activation

Activate this SOP only from an approved recall notice containing the product identifier, affected batch or date range, recall class, and effective time. AI retrieval may explain operational steps but must not invent a recall or interpret clinical risk.

## [PR-02] Immediate containment

Stop sale immediately, isolate affected stock in the designated quarantine location, prevent replenishment and transfer, and record the quantity isolated. Do not destroy or return stock until the recall coordinator issues disposition instructions.

## [PR-03] Inventory and sales reconciliation

Reconcile on-hand, sold, returned, transferred, quarantined, and unaccounted quantities for each affected store and product. Inventory adjustments must reference the recall identifier and use the authorised recall adjustment reason.

## [PR-04] Escalation and notification

Notify the area manager and recall coordinator when any affected unit is unaccounted for, remains saleable in a system, or was transferred after activation. Customer or regulator communication must use approved recall material and authorised channels.

## [PR-05] Closure evidence

Closure requires coordinator approval, complete store attestations, reconciled quantities, disposition evidence, and removal of the stop-sale control only when formally authorised. Retain all evidence under the compliance retention schedule.
